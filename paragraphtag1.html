Rohit sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer who currently captains the India national cricket team across all formats.

He is a right-handed batsman. Considered one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of the greatest opening batters of all time

Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and leadership skills. Sharma holds several batting records which famously include most sixes in international cricket
